What's Carnegie Financial Service eSavings / Online savings account?

eSavings / Online Saving account is a saving account managed and funded primarily on the Internet. eSavings / Online Savings account is often characterized by a higher interest rate or lower fees, compared with traditional savings accounts. Many of these high-yield accounts have no minimum balance. Account holders may link their eSavings / Online Savings to their existing external bank accounts for easy transfer of funds between multiple accounts. Some also offer ATM cards so customers can directly access the funds in their esavings / Online Savings account.
A high-yield savings account is another name for a savings account that typically earns a higher interest rate than your standard passbook savings account. A high-interest savings account is a wise choice if you would like to earn as much interest on your money as possible, while still retaining access to your funds. A high-yield savings account also is a good choice if you would like to shield some of your money from the volatility of the stock market. In most cases, a savings account reacts more slowly to changes in the market, so it tends to have a higher rate than even a money market account.
